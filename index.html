Our Programs

Carefully designed tracks for every stage of your child's growth.

🌺 Foundation (Ibtida'i)

Strong start in Arabic reading, writing, memorisation and morals, with Fridays' general revision.

📚 Middle (I'dadi)

Grammar, fiqh and tajweed deepened, with guided memorisation and weekly lectures.

🧑🏻‍🏫 Advanced (Thanawi)

Advanced texts, research habits and preparation for higher Islamic learning.

🕮️ Tahfeedhul-Qur'an

Evening memorisation classes - Thursdays, Fridays & Saturdays, 4:00PM till sunset.
