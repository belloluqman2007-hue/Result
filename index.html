Portal Login

Accounts are activated by the school management. Once activated, log in with your role:

Full management: results, publishing, finance, attendance, admissions.

 👩🏻‍🏫 Teacher

Score entry, attendance, exams, report sheets and class tools.

 🧑‍🏫 Student

Log in with your Student ID and surname to view your published results.

 👪 Parent

Log in with your child's Student ID and surname to follow their progress.
